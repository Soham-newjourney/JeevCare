Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 24, 2025

At JeevCare, protecting your privacy and the data relating to civic animal rescues is our highest priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our application.

Our Core Commitment: We never sell your personal data. The data collected is strictly used to facilitate rapid emergency responses, verify organizations, and improve civic analytics.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Data: Name, email address, phone number, and physical addresses (if saved in your profile).
  • Location Data: When you report an emergency, we collect precise GPS coordinates (GeoJSON 2dsphere points) from your browser/device to accurately route the distress signal to the nearest NGO.
  • Organization Data: For NGOs and Services, we collect registration numbers, licenses, operating capacity, and banking info (for the donor module).
  • Media: Photos or videos uploaded as part of an emergency report.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect in the following ways:

  • To rapidly dispatch verified NGOs to exact incident locations.
  • To facilitate direct communication (in-app chat) between the reporting citizen and the assigned NGO.
  • To generate anonymized "heatmaps" and analytics for Municipal Authorities.
  • To prevent abuse, spam, and fraudulent reporting across the network.

3. Information Sharing with Third Parties

JeevCare operates as a hub. We share necessary data (like the emergency location, description, and your contact number) with the specific **verified NGO** that accepts your case to ensure they can find the animal. Aggregated, anonymized data (stripped of personal identifiers) may be shared with municipal authorities or academic researchers via our Open Data portal.

4. Data Security

We implement robust, enterprise-grade security measures designed to protect your information. This includes military-grade bcrypt password hashing, JSON Web Tokens (JWT) for secure session management, and encrypted database connections. However, no electronic transmission over the internet or information storage technology can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you, request corrections, or request deletion ("Right to be Forgotten"). You can manage these preferences directly in your `Settings > Privacy` dashboard or by contacting privacy@jeevcare.org.
