Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the platform and reporting.

How does the routing algorithm work? +
JeevCare uses a spatial $nearSphere database query. When you report an incident with a location, we instantly query all verified NGOs within a 10km radius. We then factor in their current active caseloads to assign the case to the most available and closest responder.
Can I report anonymously? +
Yes. You do not need to register to report an emergency. However, registered Citizens earn Trust Scores for valid reports, prioritizing their tickets automatically in the queue.
How are duplicate reports handled? +
We employ a robust duplication mechanism. If a report is made for the same animal type within a 2km radius inside a 6-hour window, the new report is automatically merged into the master assignment timeline, reducing spam.
How can my NGO join? +
Click 'Join Network' and register under the NGO role. You must provide a valid registration number. A local authority will audit your profile and toggle your 'Verified' status, after which you will begin receiving real-time SOS assignments.
