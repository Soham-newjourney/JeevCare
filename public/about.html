Our Mission

Bridging the gap between distressed street animals and rapid-response organizations.

Why JeevCare Exists

Every day, countless street animals suffer due to accidents, disease, or human conflict. While there are passionate NGOs ready to help, the communication delay and manual routing of incidents costs critical time.

JeevCare was built to solve the logistical nightmare of matching a reported emergency with the closest, available, and verified rescue organization.

How We Do It

By leveraging a modern Node.js backend equipped with geospatial `$nearSphere` algorithms on MongoDB, JeevCare instantly calculates workloads and distances.

It cuts out the middleman, filters out duplicate reports algorithmically (2km / 6-hour bounds), and pings the matched NGO via Socket.io milliseconds after a citizen hits "Report".
