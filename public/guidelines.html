Community Guidelines

JeevCare is a collaborative network driven by compassion. To ensure the safety of the animals and the efficiency of our NGO partners, everyone must adhere to these standards.

🚨

Absolute Truth in Reporting

When generating an emergency SOS, accuracy is paramount. Do not exaggerate the condition to get a faster response, and absolutely never make a false report. Every deployed ambulance costs time and money; false reports will lead to permanent IP and account bans.

🤝

Respect for Rescuers & NGOs

NGOs operate under immense pressure, often with limited funding. Treat fleet drivers, shelter staff, and veterinarians with the utmost respect. Abuse, harassment, or verbal threats directed at assigned personnel will not be tolerated.

📸

Graphic Content Warning

While uploading images of injured animals aids diagnosis, refrain from uploading unnecessarily gruesome context on public community forums. Use the designated 'Emergency Report' private upload instead.

💬

Constructive Community Forums

Keep the local community boards focused on civic animal welfare (lost pets, feeding drives, adoption). Spam, commercial advertising unrelated to pet services, and political debates are prohibited.

Please Do

  • Stand by near the injured animal (at a safe distance) after reporting until help arrives if possible.
  • Provide clear, well-lit photos when generating a report to help vets assess the required equipment.
  • Update the report status to "Resolved" if the animal recovers or leaves the area before an NGO accepts the case.
  • Use the direct chat feature to guide the ambulance driver to exact coordinates if GPS is failing.

Please Don't

  • Do not attempt to move severely traumatized or aggressive injured animals yourself prior to expert arrival.
  • Do not file duplicate reports for the same incident (the system automatically flags a 2km radius anyway).
  • Do not expect instantaneous arrivals. High-priority "SOS Critical" cases (accidents/bleeding) will preempt standard illness cases in the dispatch queue.
